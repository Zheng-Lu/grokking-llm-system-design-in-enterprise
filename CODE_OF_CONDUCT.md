# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We want this repository to be a respectful, technically serious, and open environment for contributors with different backgrounds and experience levels.

Participants in this community agree to make collaboration constructive, evidence-driven, and professional.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful in reviews, issues, and discussions.
- Challenge ideas directly, not people.
- Prefer precise technical feedback over vague dismissal.
- Cite sources when making factual claims.
- Assume good intent, but do not hand-wave poor reasoning.
- Help keep the project readable and useful for future contributors.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks or deliberately hostile review behavior
- Publishing private information without permission
- Repeated bad-faith arguments that derail technical discussion
- Spam, trolling, or abusive conduct in project spaces

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to repository issues, pull requests, discussions, review comments, and any other official project communication channels.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the maintainers at `zheng.lu@gmail.com`.

Please include:

- what happened
- where it happened
- who was involved
- any relevant screenshots or links

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warning, temporary restriction, or removal from the project spaces.

The goal is to protect a productive technical environment, not to win arguments.
