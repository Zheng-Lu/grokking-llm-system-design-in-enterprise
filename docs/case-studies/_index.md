# Case Study Catalog

This section gathers public case studies and structured starter chapters for **Grokking LLM System Design in Enterprise**.

## What makes a strong case study

A strong case study should:

- teach reusable design lessons
- have enough public material to support major claims
- describe a real enterprise constraint, not only a product feature
- make trade-offs and failure modes visible

## Good future additions

Promising case study candidates often include:

- enterprise assistants with permission-aware retrieval
- internal coding or support copilots
- agent systems with explicit tool governance
- AI platform patterns reused across multiple products

## Editorial reminder

Case studies do not need perfect visibility into every implementation detail. They do need honest boundaries between sourced fact, inference, and open questions.
